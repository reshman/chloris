Chloris Designs

Chloris designs originated with Athira, a widely travelled artist who picked up her skills from various countries in Deco Clay (Singapore/ Japan), Stained Glass (Japan/ Netherlands), Photography and Canvass.

Chloris Designs specializes in creating custom, handmade, floral designs from specialized clay (Deco Clay). As the deco clay flowers are incredibly realistic and light they are much preferred by brides for bouquets, corsages, boutonniere, cake flowers, corsages etc.. The clay can be handcrafted into any flowers and colors of one’s imagination.

Experience

Athira is a freelance artist who is a Deco Clay Craft Academy Instructor.

She was fortunate to be trained under the Master Instructor – Vicky Lee from Singapore who acquired her skills from Kazuko Miyai (founder of DECO Clay Craft Academy, Japan).

Athira is a Software Engineer by profession who left her career to follow the passion of her life. Athira now spends her time making custom made flowers for various occasions, teaching Deco Clay Craft and photography. She has now started to impart training for equally interested and talented people in Deco Clay Craft.

What we do

Chloris Designs makes custom made floral arrangement, wedding bouquets, corsages, wreath and many more.

We create to the vision of the clientele of flowers, designs and arrangements that blooms in their mind. We take orders both online and from workshops/ exhibitions that are held across the globe.

About Deco Clay

Deco Clay flowers are perfect alternative to fresh flowers that can be kept lifelong without withering off like natural flowers. You always have your favorite wedding flowers to remember.

Deco Clay is eco-friendly alternative to fresh flowers and everlasting. They are light weight, durable and most importantly all beautiful and realistic.

Where to buy

You can order the flowers online or you may chose to attend any of our upcoming workshop/ exhibitions. We ship to most countries of the world. We are serving clients from USA, UK, Netherlands, Germany, Singapore, Japan, China and more.
